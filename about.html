About the Author

William Lee

The Beginning

William Lee is a writer with a passion for flawed heroes, dark conspiracies, and the kind of buried truths that refuse to stay hidden. Gone Before the Moon is his debut novel, and the first in a planned series set in a modern world where supernatural powers are emerging; alongside a creeping darkness. Inspired by a wide range of comics, TV shows, and films that explore superheroes, the supernatural, and unsolved mysteries, William brings a cinematic energy and emotional grit to his storytelling. When he’s not writing, he enjoys playing and watching football, loyally supporting Wolverhampton Wanderers, as well as game nights and all things trivia. This is just the beginning of his journey. More stories are on the way!

"No one is evil for the sake of it. Some people are simply pushed to the point where they break."

The Work

William explores stories in which no one is safe and sets those stakes right from the start. He has a completely fresh take on the supernatural genre, which ties in well with his grounded superpowers, hidden conspiracies, and secret societies. His characters aren't all heroes. Most are just like you. Navigating a world that wasn't meant for them to see.

Taking inspiration from a wide range of books, movies, and comics, Will brings a cinematic style to his writing. He has built up a world unlike any other.
